## üìù Creating Quotes with Revenue Features

### Step-by-Step Guide

#### Step 1: Create Basic Quote

1. Click "Create New Quote"
2. Select customer (or create new customer)
3. Add products to quote:
   - Search by name, SKU, or category
   - Click "Add to Quote"
   - Adjust quantities if needed
4. Apply any discounts (if authorized)

**Your quote now shows subtotal, tax, and total**

#### Step 2: Add Revenue Features

1. Click the **"Show Revenue Features"** button
2. You'll see 5 feature sections appear

#### Step 3: Add Financing (if applicable)

**The system auto-suggests financing for quotes over $1,000**

1. In the Financing section, select a plan:
   - 12 months @ 0% APR
   - 24 months @ 5.99% APR
   - 36 months @ 7.99% APR
   - 48 months @ 9.99% APR

2. Click "Calculate Monthly Payment"

3. The monthly payment displays prominently

**Sales Tip:** Lead with the monthly payment, not the total price!
‚ùå "This will cost you $2,500"
‚úÖ "You can have this for just $89/month"

#### Step 4: Add Warranties

**The system suggests warranties based on product categories**

1. Review suggested warranties for your products
2. Click checkboxes to select warranty plans
3. Duration options: 2 years, 3 years, 5 years
4. See total warranty cost added to quote

**Sales Tip:** Frame warranties as insurance
‚úÖ "For just $X more, you're covered for 5 years of worry-free ownership"

#### Step 5: Calculate Delivery

**The system suggests delivery for large/heavy items**

1. Enter customer's delivery address
2. Enter delivery distance (miles from store)
3. Select delivery time preference:
   - Standard (7-10 days)
   - Express (2-3 days)
   - Premium (next day)
4. Add floor level if apartment/condo
5. Click "Calculate Delivery Cost"

**Sales Tip:** Emphasize convenience and professional service
‚úÖ "We'll bring it right to your kitchen and set it up - no hassle for you"

#### Step 6: Apply Rebates

**The system automatically shows applicable rebates**

1. Review suggested rebates
2. Rebates apply automatically
3. Savings deducted from total

**Sales Tip:** Use rebates as a closing tool
‚úÖ "Lucky timing! This qualifies for a $500 manufacturer rebate right now"

#### Step 7: Add Trade-Ins

1. Click "Add Trade-In"
2. Describe the item (brand, model, condition)
3. Enter estimated value
4. Credit applies to quote total

**Sales Tip:** Overcome price objections
‚úÖ "What if I could give you $300 credit for your old fridge?"

#### Step 8: Review Quote

1. Review the "Applied Features Summary"
2. Check all calculations
3. Verify customer information

#### Step 9: Save or Send Quote

**Option A: Save Quote**
- Click "Save Quote"
- Quote saved with unique ID
- Access later from "My Quotes"

**Option B: Send to Customer**
- Click "Generate PDF"
- Review PDF (includes all revenue features)
- Click "Email to Customer"
- Enter customer email
- Add personalized message
- Click "Send"

## üìä Feature Details

### Financing Calculator Deep Dive

**Available Plans:**
| Term | APR | Best For |
|------|-----|----------|
| 12 mo | 0% | Promotions, quick payoff |
| 24 mo | 5.99% | Balance of cost and term |
| 36 mo | 7.99% | Larger purchases |
| 48 mo | 9.99% | Maximum affordability |

**How Monthly Payment is Calculated:**
```
For $2,000 purchase at 5.99% APR for 24 months:
Monthly Payment = $88.54/month
Total Interest = $124.96
Total Cost = $2,124.96
```

**Commission Structure:**
- You earn 1-3% commission on approved financing
- Tracked automatically in your performance dashboard

### Warranty Plans Deep Dive

**Appliance Coverage:**
- **2 years**: Parts + labor, in-home service
- **3 years**: Parts + labor, in-home service, no deductible
- **5 years**: Parts + labor, in-home service, no deductible, food loss coverage

**Electronics Coverage:**
- **2 years**: Manufacturer defects, parts + labor
- **3 years**: Defects + accidental damage, screen protection
- **5 years**: Complete coverage, no lemon guarantee

**Furniture Coverage:**
- **3 years**: Stain protection, structural damage
- **5 years**: Stain + rip protection, structural damage, cushion support

**What's Covered:**
- Mechanical/electrical failures
- Normal wear and tear (furniture)
- Accidental damage (electronics)
- Cosmetic damage from accidents (furniture)

**What's NOT Covered:**
- Intentional damage
- Cosmetic issues (appliances/electronics)
- Pre-existing conditions

### Delivery Services Deep Dive

**Standard Delivery Includes:**
- Pick up from store/warehouse
- Transport to customer address
- Bring into home
- Unbox and remove packaging
- Basic setup/positioning

**Premium Delivery Adds:**
- Installation/hookup (appliances)
- Level and anchor (furniture)
- Old unit removal and haul-away
- Cleaning of installation area

**Delivery Pricing Factors:**
- Distance from store
- Item size and weight
- Floor level (stairs/elevator)
- Time preference (standard/express/premium)
- Additional services (installation, removal)

### Trade-In Evaluation Guide

**Condition Assessment:**
- **Excellent**: Like new, no visible wear, < 2 years old
- **Good**: Minor wear, fully functional, 2-5 years old
- **Fair**: Moderate wear, fully functional, 5-10 years old
- **Poor**: Heavy wear, may have issues, > 10 years old

**Value Guidelines:**
| Condition | % of Original Price | Example (orig $1,000) |
|-----------|--------------------|-----------------------|
| Excellent | 40-60% | $400-$600 |
| Good | 20-40% | $200-$400 |
| Fair | 10-20% | $100-$200 |
| Poor | 5-10% | $50-$100 |

**Items We Accept:**
- Appliances (fridges, washers, dryers, ranges)
- Furniture (sofas, beds, dining sets)
- Electronics (TVs, receivers)

**Items We DON'T Accept:**
- Non-functional items
- Items over 15 years old
- Damaged beyond repair
- Recalled products

## üîß Troubleshooting

### Issue: Financing calculator not working
**Solution:**
- Ensure quote total is > $0
- Refresh the page and try again
- Check internet connection
- Contact IT support if persists

### Issue: Delivery calculator shows error
**Solution:**
- Verify address is complete and valid
- Check distance is entered correctly
- Ensure delivery service is active in system
- Try different delivery option

### Issue: Warranties not showing for product
**Solution:**
- Verify product category is set correctly
- Check product price meets minimum ($200+)
- Confirm warranties are loaded in system
- Contact manager to add warranty plans

### Issue: Cannot send email to customer
**Solution:**
- Verify customer email address is correct
- Check email format (must include @)
- Confirm internet connection
- Generate PDF first, then send
- Contact IT if email service is down

### Issue: Trade-in value seems incorrect
**Solution:**
- Double-check condition assessment
- Verify similar items in trade-in database
- Consult with manager for unusual items
- Document reason for final value given

## ‚ùì FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Do I have to use revenue features on every quote?**
**A:** Not required, but highly recommended. Features increase your commissions and help customers get better value.

**Q: How do I access my past quotes?**
**A:** Click "My Quotes" in the navigation. You can search, filter, and sort your quote history.

**Q: Can I edit a quote after saving?**
**A:** Yes! Open the quote from "My Quotes" and click "Edit". Make changes and save again.

**Q: What happens if a customer declines financing?**
**A:** No problem - just uncheck the financing option and the quote recalculates without it.

**Q: Can I combine multiple rebates?**
**A:** Usually yes, unless specifically stated otherwise. System will flag conflicts.

### Revenue Features FAQ

**Q: How long does financing approval take?**
**A:** Usually 5-15 minutes. We can complete the application while writing up the quote.

**Q: What if the customer's financing is declined?**
**A:** Remove financing from quote and discuss alternative payment options or smaller purchase.

**Q: Do warranties transfer if customer sells the item?**
**A:** Most warranties are transferable to new owners. Check specific plan details.

**Q: Can customer cancel warranty later?**
**A:** Yes, within 30 days for full refund. After that, prorated refund available.

**Q: How far do we deliver?**
**A:** Up to 100 miles from store. Beyond that, contact logistics for special arrangement.

**Q: What if delivery date needs to change?**
**A:** Contact delivery team at least 24 hours in advance to reschedule.

**Q: Do rebates expire?**
**A:** Yes - each rebate has start and end dates. System only shows active rebates.

**Q: Can we apply a rebate retroactively?**
**A:** Within 30 days of purchase, yes. Contact customer service to process.

**Q: What if we overestimated trade-in value?**
**A:** Actual trade-in value assessed at pickup. If significantly different, customer pays difference or keeps old item.

### Analytics & Reporting

**Q: How do I see my revenue feature performance?**
**A:** Click "Analytics" in navigation (if you have access). View adoption rates and revenue by feature.

**Q: Do revenue features count toward my sales goals?**
**A:** Yes! Warranties and delivery count as product sales. Financing generates separate commission.

**Q: Can I see which features perform best?**
**A:** Yes - Analytics dashboard shows top-performing features across all sales reps.
